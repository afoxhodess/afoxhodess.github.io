Ana Fox-Hodess

Ana Fox-Hodess (1988 Berkeley) is California-based artist. Her work engages with the relationship between media, memory and history; voyeurism and exhibitionism in the digital age; and the limits between craft and new media technologies. Principal research interests include participatory art, interactivity, and the construction of non-linear narratives.
